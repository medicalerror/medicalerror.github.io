A Sadly Welcome to Medical Malpractice

Published: Fri Sep 20 2019

Sadly, welcome to Medical Malpractice where I will disclose my medical error experience due to medical malpractice that leads me to develop a disease. I wrecked a lot medical bills hoping to get better, but those medical malpractitioners just ended up damaging my kidneys, leaving me with a disease.

It's very heart breaking because the harm that's caused by their medical error can be prevented if it was done with safety. If only those unprofessional healthcare providers do their jobs carefully, this would never happen. It seems that they don't really have care on their patients and couldn't feel what their patients feel, no matter what kind of danger will going to happen to their patients.

Please share for other people's awareness

If I only have an opportunity, I would give those greedy and careless malpractitioners some lessons.
